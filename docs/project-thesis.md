# Project Thesis

QuantFactory V3 is a federated LLM-methodology research portfolio for trading intelligence.

Its purpose is to discover how messy trader source material can be ingested, extracted, synthesized, evaluated, and transformed into useful downstream artifacts.

V3 is not one fixed pipeline. V3 is a research federation for comparing many possible pipelines.

---

## Source Material

V3 is designed for messy and varied trading material:

- trader education text,
- course transcripts,
- video captions,
- chart walkthroughs,
- screenshots and frames,
- books and PDFs,
- notes and journals,
- examples and stories,
- market context,
- future benchmark packs.

The goal is to learn which methods work for which downstream uses.

---

## Downstream Possibilities

V3 is allowed to explore many downstream artifact types, including:

- trading judgment principles,
- study cards,
- concept clusters,
- contradiction maps,
- source examples,
- formula candidates,
- data requirement maps,
- visual/deictic bindings,
- research tasks,
- source-native strategy candidates,
- cross-source hypotheses,
- playbook candidates,
- operator review artifacts,
- and future V4 product surfaces.

These are possibilities, not starting assumptions.

---

## Methodological Pluralism

No single ingestion or extraction method is canonical by default.

V2’s LLM-proposal-to-Python-review pattern is one possible method family, not V3 doctrine.

V3 should support experiments with:

- long-context readers,
- chunked source-span readers,
- multi-agent readers,
- example-to-principle readers,
- contradiction miners,
- concept graph induction,
- formula source readers,
- visual/deictic readers,
- model-judge comparisons,
- human-seeded expansion,
- and other future LLM-centered methods.

---

## Core Research Belief

Different downstream uses may require different upstream processes.

A method that works for study cards may fail for formula recovery.

A method that works for exact source grounding may lose broader trader judgment.

A method that works for visual/deictic binding may require video-frame context that text-only methods cannot supply.

V3 should discover these differences instead of forcing one universal pipeline.

---

## V3 To V4

V3 is the discovery federation.

V4 will be the product or hardened system that inherits what V3 proves useful.

V4 should not inherit every V3 experiment.

V4 should inherit only graduated methods, schemas, artifact types, evaluations, and product surfaces.
