# Research Question

Can an LLM judge, given blinded inputs and the same rubric, reproduce the operator-lineage manual content-review and strict line-range-review verdicts on existing live-pilot artifacts well enough to be trusted as a calibrated first-pass evaluator — and on which review axes?

Current note: this question is represented by scaffold fixture exports, a pre-registered admission packet, and proposal-only live pilot records from blinded judge-agreement trials. These records are research artifacts only. Judge verdicts never override human EvaluationRecords. They do not create review authority, validation, product authority, strategy evidence, financial advice, live-trading authority, graduation, or architecture.
